A website, a game, educating about pandemics

ABOUT

Since January 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic has taken over our lives with active cases across the globe today. With no sign of daily infections dropping, what can we do to protect our family members and ourselves from the virus?

THE GAME

A fun and interactive quiz-based game, educate yourself on what COVID-19 is, where it first originated from, and how it has impacted our lives this past 1 year.

Learn more about what a pandemic is and how we can ensure that a future pandemic does not happen again.

EARN REWARDS

Gain points by completing different quizzes, spend the points in the Points Shop and redeem basic necessities during this pandemic such as a pack of 50 surgical masks and more!
